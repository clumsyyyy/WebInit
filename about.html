Background

In today’s world, technology plays a big role in all aspects of our daily life. With technology changing the world exponentially, many transformations are changing the world digitally. These transformations automatically drive up the demand for digital talents.

Digital talents are required for a person to adapt and utilize existing digital technologies in a rapidly changing world. Digital talents are also important to aid activities and provide information for decision-making processes of an organization or a company to achieve its objectives. However, developing digital talents is still not a priority despite the huge demand.

The low number of digital talents is a challenge that Indonesia has to face and solve in the face of a digital era during the Fourth Industrial Revolution. According to the IMD World Index, Indonesia placed 56th out of 63 countries in terms of the country’s preparedness in using digital technology as its main drive in economic transformation.

Who’s Init?

We are the batch 2020 students of Information Systems & Technology and Informatics Engineering students at Institut Teknologi Bandung. We are present in the community to provide general knowledge about technology through talk shows, programming bootcamps, and other educational content on social media to support the growth of digital talents.

Why Init?

By definition, init (short for initialization) is the first process started during the booting of the computer system and is a daemon process that continues running until the system is shut down. It’s just like us! We are the first generation of students to experience the world of informatics via online, from online meetings to online classes.

What’s the Initiator?

The IniTiator is an action by INIT 2020 as a concrete step to introduce digital technology, especially for college students, to support the growth of digital talent in Indonesia.

Our goals through The IniTiator are:
  • To introduce the digital technology to Indonesian society
  • To educate people about the soft and hard skills needed in the world of digital technology and its industry
  • To help people in need through donation

To achieve those goals, we are creating interesting educational contents through Instagram and YouTube.

We have also prepared series of events such as talkshows or bootcamps with the aim of solving the digital talent issue. Stay tuned!
